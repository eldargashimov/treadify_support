What to Include

To help us resolve your inquiry more efficiently, please include the following information in your email:

  • A clear subject line (e.g., "Treadify Support: [Brief Summary]").
  • A detailed description of your question, suggestion, or the issue you are experiencing.
  • The type of device you are using (e.g., iPhone 13, iPad Air).
  • The version of iOS installed on your device.

We strive to respond to all legitimate support inquiries within 48 hours on business days. Thank you for using Treadify!
